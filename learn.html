Methods

  • Excavation: Systematic digging layer by layer.
  • Stratigraphy: Analyzing soil layers to date objects.
  • Carbon Dating: Measuring radioactive decay in organic material.

Decolonial Archaeology

Modern archaeologists challenge colonial-era practices by including Indigenous voices, respecting sacred sites, and acknowledging that heritage belongs to the communities it represents.
